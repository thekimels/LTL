ABOUT US

We provide Logistics and Transportation Solutions to clients
in Southern Ontario, Specializing in the Greater Toronto Area

1

SERVICE FLEXIBILITY

Logistic Transportation Limited provides and maintains a general fleet of specialized equipment and an on-call driver pool throughout our service region. Our ability to meet volume surges provides a significant benefit to our customers who experience the cost advantage of matching their fluctuating trucking requirements with our cost-effective pricing policies.

2

OPERATIONAL STABILITY

Our well-trained and courteous staff provide excellent customer service. Our equipment is well maintained, and all our drivers are covered by the highest levels of insurance available. Our seasoned management professionals apply best practices to deliver the business results you expect by making sure your goods arrive on time, undamaged and correct every time.

3

TRANSPORTATION SAFETY

All efforts are made to ensure the safety of our employees and the safe transportation of your goods.
We employ mandatory staff and broker health & safety training, a detailed driver's procedure manual in every vehicle, and maintain a consistent CVOR safety rating with the Ontario Ministry of Transportation. All of our brokers, drivers, helpers and warehouse staff are covered by WSIB and we have a zero-tolerance policy on drugs and alcohol.
